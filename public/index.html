Bible Plan RSS Feed Generator

Simple to use RSS Feed Generator for reading a specified number of chapters per day from a feed reader such as Feedly or the likes.

How to use

Simply build RSS feed url like the below examples. You simply specify the plan, start date, and number of chapters you want to have added to to your RSS Feed each day.


Examples

Notes:Dates are in yyyyMMdd format so June 5th, 2021 = 20210605
  • Old Testament Only with 1 chapter a day starting with 2022-01-01: https://www.tryomas.com/rssbible/ot/20220101/1/feed.rss
  • New Testament Only with 1 chapter a day starting with 2022-01-01: https://www.tryomas.com/rssbible/nt/20220101/1/feed.rss
  • Full bible with 1 chapter a day starting with 2022-01-01: https://www.tryomas.com/rssbible/full/20220101/1/feed.rss
  • Full bible with 5 chapter a day starting with 2022-01-01: https://www.tryomas.com/rssbible/full/20220101/5/feed.rss
